Mike's Easy Burger Sauce

Amplify your burger with this creamy concoction!

This recipe stemmed from my exploration of smash burgers. I knew something was missing when I made them... But what?

The sauce! No matter what I did, I neglected the most important thing. If you don't have a good sauce, your burger will be lackluster.

Give this one a shot. You won't regret it.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 of a small onion, grated
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Hot Sauce
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Start the base of your sauce with a majority of it being kewpie mayo, place in bowl
  2. Add hot sauce, to taste
  3. Add a light amount of Worcestershire sauce. The more you add, the more smokey.
  4. Add a small amount of mustard, to taste. The more you add, the more tart.
  5. (optional) Add a light amount of ketchup if you like your sauce on the sweeter end.
  6. Grate your 1/4 onion finely, where its almost all juice and a lump of onion into the sauce.
  7. Stir ingredients together, adjust to taste as needed. Can be stored in the fridge for as long as your mayo lasts!
